Northern Atelier needed a sharper online presence without taking on a heavy CMS or a JavaScript-heavy frontend.

The work focused on three goals:

1. Clarify the studio's positioning in the first screen.
2. Turn project pages into richer case studies.
3. Make blog-style updates easy enough for non-technical editors.

The final result pairs a warm editorial visual system with a fast static Astro build. Decap CMS handles simple publishing while keeping the content Git-friendly for the development team.
