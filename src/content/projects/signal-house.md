Signal House had strong expertise but the original site buried it under generic copy and disconnected sections.

This redesign simplified the journey:

- a high-confidence hero with a specific value proposition
- modular sections for service pillars
- proof-driven project highlights
- a lighter contact path for inbound leads

The result feels more like a premium consulting brand and less like a placeholder template.
